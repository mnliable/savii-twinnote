BIOGRAPHY

Savii Nova is a singer, songwriter, dancer, and actor, born in Manchester and raised on rhythm. Starting out as a competitive dancer, she naturally transitioned into acting, but music has always been the driving force behind her artistic journey. A self-taught musician on guitar, ukulele, and piano, she discovered a passion for songwriting early on—and has been hooked ever since.

Her sound lives at the intersection of pop and dance: emotional lyrics you can move to. With a wide vocal range and a deep love for storytelling, she creates music that resonates with both the heart and the hips. Her dance background brings a unique kinetic energy to every performance, whether on stage or in the studio.

At the core of her work is a desire for connection. Through her music, she aims to make listeners feel seen, uplifted, and supported in whatever they're going through.
